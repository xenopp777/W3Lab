Threes Away

Score the lowest value of points possible!

Game Setup

Rules & How to Play:

  • 3s count as zero, all other dice count as their face value.
  • Each player rolls 5 dice and must hold at least one die per roll. All 3s must be held.
  • Reroll until all five dice are held. Your score is the sum of the dice.
  • The player with the lowest score wins!
